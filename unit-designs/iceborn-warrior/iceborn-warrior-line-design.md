### **Key Features**
- **High XP Requirements**: 40-50% higher experience costs than standard factions
- **Late Game Power**: Level 4 units significantly outpower other faction equivalents
- **Magical Attacks**: High-level units bypass resistances with magical damage
- **Leadership Abilities**: Chieftains and Warlords provide army-wide bonuses

### **Tactical Role**
Primary melee infantry, heavy frontline fighters, army leadership in advanced forms.

### **Lore**
Hardy fighters trained to survive and fight in the harshest conditions, evolving into legendary commanders who inspire entire armies.

### **Lore and Background**
*In the harshest regions of the frozen north, where even the hardy Frost Clans struggle to survive, live the great ice bears - massive creatures perfectly adapted to the eternal winter. These intelligent beasts are revered by the clans as sacred animals, symbols of strength and endurance.*

*Only the most skilled and respected warriors earn the right to approach an ice bear, and fewer still manage to form the sacred bond that creates a Bear Rider partnership. This bond is more than mere domestication - it is a spiritual connection forged through mutual respect, shared hardship, and unwavering trust.*

*Bear Riders are not simply cavalry - they are living legends, warriors who have proven their worth in the eyes of both clan and beast. In battle, rider and mount fight as one, the warrior's tactical knowledge combined with the bear's primal fury creating a force that can turn the tide of any engagement.*

### **Cultural Significance**
- **Sacred Bond**: The partnership represents the highest achievement in Frost Clans culture
- **Survival Mastery**: Only those who have mastered arctic survival can approach the bears
- **Leadership Role**: Bear Riders often serve as war chiefs and expedition leaders
- **Spiritual Element**: The bond is considered blessed by the spirits of winter

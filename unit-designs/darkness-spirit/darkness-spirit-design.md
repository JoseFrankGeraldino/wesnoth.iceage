# Darkness Spirit Unit Line Design - Ice Dwellers

## Overview
The Darkness Spirit embodies shadow magic and void forces channeled by Ice Dwellers shamans. These mysterious entities bring deception, debuffs, and mystical damage to the battlefield, serving as versatile attackers with unique crowd-manipulation capabilities. Darkness Spirits represent the darker aspects of the Ice Dwellers faction.

## Unit Progression

### Shadow Wisp (Level 1)
**Cost:** 18 Gold | **HP:** 19 | **Experience:** 28

**Appearance:**
- Amorphous shadow form with ethereal wisps
- Deep purple-black coloration with violet highlights
- Shifting, unstable appearance with smoke-like trails
- Size: 64×64 sprite base

**Abilities:**
- Shadow Bolt: Single target attack with mystical damage
- Obscure: Creates concealment aura affecting nearby units
- Void Affinity: Resistance to elemental damage

**Movement:** 6 tiles/turn
**Attack Range:** 2-4 tiles (ranged)
**Resistances:** Void/Shadow (partial immunity)

---

### Umbral Spirit (Level 2)
**Cost:** 36 Gold | **Experience:** 60

**Progression from Shadow Wisp:**
- Larger, more defined shadow form
- Enhanced purple-black aura with violet energy
- More menacing appearance with tendrils of shadow

**Enhanced Abilities:**
- Void Strike: Ranged attack penetrating magical defenses
- Shadow Grasp: Reduces enemy evasion and initiative
- Darkness Veil: Creates zones of shadow affecting visibility

**Movement:** 6 tiles/turn
**Attack Range:** 3-5 tiles (ranged)

---

### Void Avatar (Level 3)
**Cost:** 54 Gold

**Final Form Appearance:**
- Massive shadowy entity wreathed in void energy
- Complex pattern of shadow and violet light
- Piercing void-core with hypnotic dark aurora effects
- Size: 96×96 sprite base

**Ultimate Abilities:**
- Void Rend: Massive mystical attack ignoring defense
- Endless Night: Creates persistent shadow zones
- Soul Drain: Damages enemies while healing self

**Movement:** 5 tiles/turn
**Attack Range:** 4-6 tiles

---

## Special Mechanics

### Affiliation
- Neutral/Independent alignment
- Works with any faction but has unique interaction with darkness-themed units
- Can operate independently or be integrated into strategic formations

### Advantage Systems
- Strong against magical units and spirit-type enemies
- Excels in low-light/nighttime conditions
- Debuff abilities stack with other crowd-control effects

### Weakness
- Holy/light-based attacks deal increased damage
- Reduced effectiveness in bright light/daytime
- Vulnerable to cleansing and anti-magic effects

---

## Visual Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: Deep Purple (#4B0082), Black (#000000)
- Secondary: Violet (#8B00FF), Dark Gray (#2F4F4F)
- Glow: Purple-black gradient with void effects

### Animation States
- Idle: Gentle swirling shadow with pulsing void center
- Attack: Sudden dark burst with trailing shadow
- Hit: Dissipation and reformation animation
- Death: Fading into absolute darkness

---

## Combat Role

### Strengths
- Excellent debuff and crowd-control capabilities
- Works well in mixed squad compositions
- Strong mystical damage against magical enemies

### Strategic Positioning
- Adaptable to multiple roles (support, control, damage)
- Complements both fire and ice spirits
- Useful for tactical positioning through debuffs

---

## Faction Integration

### Primary Alignment
- Represents the neutral/shadow forces of the realm
- Can be deployed as mercenary units
- Offer unique tactical options through debuff abilities

### Synergies
- Works with any unit type for diverse strategies
- Particularly effective against elite/magical enemies
- Provides tactical flexibility in team compositions

---

## Balance Notes
- Balanced damage with significant utility through debuffs
- Moderate defensive stats with magical resilience
- Debuff effects provide strategic value beyond pure damage
- Creates interesting counter-play through light-based opposition
- Fills unique niche in trinity of elemental spirits (Fire/Ice/Darkness)

## **Tactical Implications**

### **Twilight Units**:
- Excel at transition moments (dawn/dusk attacks)
- Good for defensive positioning and timing attacks  
- Perfect for diplomatic scenarios and support roles
- Require careful turn planning for maximum effectiveness

### **Solaris-Noctis Units**:
- Dominate during peak power times (midday blazing sun, midnight deepest cold)
- Excellent for decisive battles at extreme times
- Vulnerable during balanced transition periods (dawn/dusk)
- Natural units of absolute extremes: pure fire elementals, deep ice wraiths
- Best for all-or-nothing tactical strikes

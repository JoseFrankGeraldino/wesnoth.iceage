The Frost Clans follow a unique progression system where units require **higher experience** to level up but become **significantly more powerful** than other factions at high levels. This creates a faction that rewards patience and tactical play with devastating late-game potential.

## üéÆ **Strategic Implications**

### **Early Game Challenges**
- **Slower Progression**: Units level up 40-50% slower than other factions
- **Higher Investment**: Each veteran unit requires more battle experience
- **Risk Management**: Losing experienced units is more costly

### **Mid Game Advantage**
- **Quality over Quantity**: Fewer but more powerful units
- **Specialized Roles**: Different alignments create tactical flexibility
- **Terrain Mastery**: Bear units dominate ice/forest environments

### **Late Game Dominance**
- **Superior Stats**: Level 3-4 units significantly outpower other factions
- **Magical Attacks**: High-level units bypass resistances
- **Leadership Stacking**: Multiple commanders create army-wide bonuses
- **Unique Abilities**: Regeneration, terrifying, perfect accuracy

## ‚öîÔ∏è **Tactical Synergies**

### **Alignment Combinations**
- **Solaris-Noctis Units**: Peak power at midday/midnight (Infantry + Bears)
- **Twilight Units**: Peak power at dawn/dusk (Scouts + Cavalry)
- **Mixed Armies**: Cover all time periods with complementary units

### **Formation Strategies**
- **Bear Wall**: Heavy Bear units form defensive line with snow camouflage
- **Cavalry Sweep**: Fast Frost Cavaliers exploit flanks during twilight hours
- **Archer Support**: Pathfinders provide perfect accuracy covering fire
- **Command Structure**: Warlords and Bear Lords provide leadership coverage

### **Terrain Exploitation**
- **Ice/Forest Maps**: Bear units gain massive mobility advantage
- **Open Plains**: Cavalry units achieve maximum speed potential  
- **Mixed Terrain**: Scouts utilize concealment and skirmisher abilities

## üèÜ **Faction Identity Achievement**

This progression system creates a unique faction identity:

1. **High Risk, High Reward**: Requires patience and skill but delivers superior late-game power
2. **Tactical Depth**: Multiple alignment types force time-based strategic decisions  
3. **Veteran Focus**: Emphasizes keeping units alive to unlock their full potential
4. **Scaling Advantage**: Becomes stronger relative to other factions as games progress
5. **Thematic Consistency**: Harsh training mirrors harsh arctic environment

The Frost Clans reward players who master defensive positioning, timing management, and long-term strategic planning with access to some of the most powerful units in the game.
